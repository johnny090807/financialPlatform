FAQ'S

What is this platform about?

•  Welcome to Plugge Capital's financial platform! Plugge Captial help young enterpreuers do their financial working. You can input your invoices to the platform and see whether your financial status is good in more easily way. Also, you can check your assets and liabilities(tax to pay etc.). If you're curious about our company, You can find more information in www.pluggecapital.com

What is the overall usage of the platform?

• If you click "Invoice tables", You can input invoice information of company your dealing with. Write down Company name, Starting Balance, And Starting/End date. If you click the card on the left side, you can add your invoices here. Please click icons on the right side if that invoice or VAT has not been paid yet. And it will also show you an alert one week before you have pay Invoice/VAT.

Where can I check my invoices?

• You can check your invoices by following :
  Invices tables > Invoice you made > Overview

What can I check in each tab?

• Overview:
  You can add or delete or edit your invoices and also checking it.
• Balance :
  It shows your balances according to invoices you enter.
• Charts :
  You can see if your company is operating in healty way. You can see your Profit analysis, Income and Expenses for each month, and also where you spend most etc.
• Memorial :
  You can see your invoices not have been paid yet
• Memorial | VAT :
  You can see your VAT not have been paid yet

What currency is applied for in this platform?

• The currency used here is EUR.
